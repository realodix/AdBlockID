## AdGuard Browser Extension
1. Buka halaman *dashboard*.
2. Klik tab *Filters*. <br>

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/Vi05AGV.png) <br>

3. *Scroll* ke bawah, lalu klik *Language-specific*. <br>

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/3SghRl0.png) <br>

4. Aktifkan <span style="color:#67B279"><b>AdBlockID</b></span> <br>

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/DUAsFzL.png) <br>

<br><br>

## AdGuard for Windows (v8+)
1. Buka aplikasi AdGuard.
2. Buka halaman *Protection*.
3. Klik *Ad blocking*. <br>

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/6si6MA5.png) <br>

4. Klik *Language-specific ad blocking*.
5. Aktifkan filter berbahasa Indonesia. <br>

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/jMhTSQ2.png) <br>

<br><br>

## AdGuard for Windows (v7)
1. Buka aplikasi AdGuard.
2. Buka *Settings*.
3. Buka *Ad Blocking*.
4. Klik *Filter editor*. <br>

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/7y2MH7o.png) <br>

5. Cari **AdBlockID**, lalu centang.
6. Klik *Add selected*. <br>

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/BiAVZpD.png) <br>

<br><br>

## AdGuard for Android

### # Cara 1
- Buka halaman `Protection`.
- Klik `Ad bloking` -> `Language-specific ad blocking` <br>

   <img src="https://i.imgur.com/rvgEaik.jpg" width="600"/> <br>

### # Cara 2
- Buka halaman `Settings`.
- Klik `Filtering` -> `Filters` -> `Language-specific filters`
- Aktifkan <span style="color:#67B279"><b>AdBlockID</b></span> <br>

   ![](https://i.imgur.com/T2zbEQi.png)
